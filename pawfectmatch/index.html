About Us

Driven by an unwavering commitment to encourage adoption over purchasing, Pawfect Match has been successfully operating since its launch in 2003. Our platform serves as a medium for pet lovers to find their forever friend, and for our rescued animals to find their forever home. To achieve our vision of a world without abandoned pets or stray animals, we will continue working hard to provide a new home, a new family, and a new beginning for every animal in our care.
